SIMPLE FOOD DONE WELL

Simplicity Catering is a full service catering company in Calgary. Simplicity isn’t just our name, it’s our philosophy. We specialize in “simple food done well” and make event planning easy so that instead of planning, you’re enjoying unforgettable moments celebrating alongside your guests. We take care of everything so that your guests feel at home at your parties, weddings and corporate events or on set as your craft service providers.
